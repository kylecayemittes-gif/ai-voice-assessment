## PART 9: OBJECTION HANDLING

### Common Objections & Responses

#### **Objection 1: "We already use [Competitor] and it works fine"**

**If Skilljar/Thought Industries:**
> "That's great that [Competitor] handles your e-commerce well. What we're hearing from organizations like yours is they need to prove skills, not just completion. How do you currently demonstrate to employers or stakeholders that learners actually mastered the competencies?"

**Proof point:** Show Lightcast skills vs. custom skills, Voice Assessments vs. quizzes

**If Cornerstone/SAP (Government):**
> "Cornerstone is strong for compliance. What we're hearing from agencies like yours is they want one platform for compliance AND career development. How are you currently handling the upskilling side—the 50% that isn't mandatory compliance?"

**Proof point:** Show Learning Circles for engagement, Pathways for career development

#### **Objection 2: "We don't have budget this year"**

**Business Response:**
> "I understand budget constraints. Can we talk about turnover costs? If training improves retention by even 5%, what's that worth given your cost of replacing [role]?"

**Government Response:**
> "I understand—government budgets are tight. Can we talk about the cost of non-compliance? One lawsuit or regulatory penalty could dwarf the cost of the LMS. Plus, with FedRAMP certification, you're future-proofed for federal requirements."

#### **Objection 3: "We need FedRAMP and you don't have it"**

**Honest Response:**
> "You're right, we don't have FedRAMP certification today, which limits some federal opportunities. However, for [State/Local agencies], FedRAMP isn't required. Can we explore whether your specific agency requires it, or if other security frameworks (SOC 2, ISO 27001) meet your needs?"

**Alternative if they're flexible:**
> "While we're working toward FedRAMP, many government agencies like [Defense Language Institute, Arkansas Dept of Career Education] use Canvas successfully. Could we start with a pilot in an area that doesn't require FedRAMP while we complete certification?"

#### **Objection 4: "Your pricing is too high"**

**Value-Based Response:**
> "Let's talk about outcomes instead of pricing. If we improve completion rates from 30% to 75%, what's that worth in terms of [job placements / retention / compliance]? The ROI typically pays back within [timeframe]."

**For Government:**
> "Compared to custom solutions that many agencies build, we're actually cost-effective. Plus, you get ongoing innovation—new features like [Voice Assessments, AI Copilot] without additional development costs."
